Delete Your VibeCatch Account

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at support@vibecatch.com with your account username.
  2. Specify that you want your account and associated data deleted.
  3. We will delete your account and all related data (username, email, mood history) within 7 days.
  4. Anonymous analytics data may be retained for app improvement, but it cannot identify you personally.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@vibecatch.com.
